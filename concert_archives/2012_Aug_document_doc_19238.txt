- Notes -
Mozart (1756 – 1791) Born Salzburg.
This single movement piece one of many unfinished Mozart works. Completed by Mozart’s friend, Maximilian Stadler.
Today played by Mick and Kian.
Chopin (1810  – 1849) Polish composer.
This short piece one of his most loved tunes. Performed today by Sally and Nick.
Haydn (1732 – 1809) Austrian composer.
This work composed 1785 as part of a commission from his publisher. One of around 40 surviving piano trios. Arranged for modern instruments by Nick Stokes 2012.
Brahms (1833  – 1897) German composer.
This sonata is the last of Brahms’ three violin sonatas. Completed by 1887. Today played by Tracey and Benjamin
Piazzolla (1921 – 1992) Argentinian composer and master of the bandoneón.
This piece part of a set of four pieces representing the development of tango music over the last 100 years.
Played today by Vania & Nick.
Eesperé (1953 - ) Estonian composer.
Studied with Aram Khachaturian. Very much active today producing music with a spiritual dimension.  This final piece played by Vania, Nick & Debra
