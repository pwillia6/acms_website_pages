Beethoven (1770 – 1827)
This early work is one of the first classical cello sonatas and still a
staple of the repertoire.
Today played by Robyn and Nick.
Brahms (1833 – 1897)
Brahms’ 2nd piano trio in C, completed in 1882. 2nd movement
performed today. Takes the form of a theme and variations
Inge joins Robyn and Nick.

Piano ballades are elaborate one-section works which are
considered an innovation of Chopin's. Chopin's ballades have a
loose form and themes which are narrative in character. The third
ballade in A-flat major was published in 1841, with a dedication to
the composer's pupil, Pauline de Noailles.
Played by Iona Luke.
Schubert (1797 – 1828)
This string quartet is nicknamed ‘Rosamunde’ because of a tune
taken from Schubert’s famous incidental music for a play. Also
features other tunes used by Schubert in songs.
Today played by Stephen, Greta, Danny and Nicole.
